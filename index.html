OUR MENU

chicken mandhi

Chicken Mandi is a traditional Yemeni dish that has gained popularity across the Middle East and beyond. It consists of tender, spiced chicken served over fragrant basmati rice. The dish is known for its unique smoky flavor, achieved by cooking the meat in a tandoor or underground oven. Mandi is often served with a side of yogurt sauce, salad, or spicy tomato salsa (known as sahawiq).

alfalm mandhi

Al Fahm Mandi is a variation of the traditional Mandi, combining the flavors of grilled or roasted chicken (Al Fahm) with the fragrant spiced rice of Mandi. This version is popular in many Middle Eastern and South Asian regions. The chicken is typically marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture, then grilled or roasted to perfection, and served over a bed of aromatic Mandi rice.

shawai mandhi

Shawai Mandi is another delicious variation of the traditional Yemeni Mandi. It combines the unique flavor of Shawai chicken (grilled or roasted) with the aromatic Mandi rice. Shawai chicken is marinated in a blend of spices, then grilled or roasted, giving it a smoky and charred flavor, similar to tandoori or barbecue chicken.

spicy mandhi

Spicy Mandi is a fiery version of the traditional Mandi dish, ideal for those who enjoy bold, spicy flavors. This variation uses extra spices and chili to bring heat to both the chicken and the rice, while maintaining the essential smoky, aromatic qualities of Mandi .

Fish mandhi

Fish Mandi is a delightful seafood variation of the traditional Mandi. Instead of chicken or lamb, fresh fish is marinated with spices and grilled or fried. The flavorful fish is served over spiced Mandi rice, making it a lighter yet flavorful option

Beef mandhi

Beef Mandi is a flavorful variation of traditional Mandi, where tender, slow-cooked beef is served over spiced Mandi rice. This version is hearty and rich, with the beef absorbing the aromatic spices, making it a perfect dish for special occasions or family gatherings.
